Bangladesh Map

Bangladesh is a country located in South Asia, bordered by India to the west, north, and east, and by Myanmar to the southeast. It has a diverse geography, with the Bay of Bengal forming its southern coastline. The country is known for its fertile delta region formed by the Ganges, Brahmaputra, and Meghna rivers. Bangladesh is divided into 64 districts, each with its own administrative headquarters.
